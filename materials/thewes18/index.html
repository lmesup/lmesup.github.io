Verzerrte Beteiligung. Legitimation von Volksentscheiden am Beispiel Stuttgart 21

This is the documentation page for "Christoph Thewes (2018): Verzerrte Beteiligung. Legitimation von Volksentscheiden am Beispiel Stuttgart 21. KZfSS - Kölner Zeitschrift für Soziologie und Sozialpsychologie, 70(1)".

On this page you may found all Stata do-files for the analyses in the paper.

With the master do-file you can replicate all analyses of the paper. For this you need to download all do-files and the data. Important: If data is not publicly accessible, you'll find a note in the corresponding folder on how to obtain access.

In what follows you find the complete list of the do-files. They appear in the order of their making during the preparation of the paper.


00pr_maps.do Prepare Shape-Files
00cr_makro.do Prepare Makro-Data
00cr_s21.do Prepare S21-Election-Data
00cr_elections.do Prepare Elections (BTW 2009, LTW 2011)

01cr_ind.do Prepare Individual-Data from Gabriel/Faas/ZA5592/ZA5625
01an_reg_diag.do Regression-diagnostic

02cr_hat.do Predict counterfactual voting

03gr_maps_bw.do Create Descriptiv Maps

04gr_maps_y.do Create Bias-Maps
04gr_scatter_y.do Create Scatterplot with other participation-methods
04gr_robustness.do Create robustness check: Map+Scatter
